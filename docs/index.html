105 Keefer Opposition Letter Generator


Save Chinatown Heritage created this custom letter generator to help you get started on your 105 Keefer opposition letter to the City of Vancouver. This is not a form petition, but a way to get you over your writer's block. The due date for your comments to be included in the Staff Report to City Council is Monday, February 27, 2017.
